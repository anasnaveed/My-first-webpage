Who am I ?


I am an individual. I am my own person; a person who makes mistakes, laughs, cries, hurts, smiles and loves. I am who I am through my joys, sorrows, heartbreaks, happiness and my loses. Each step in my life and each moment that goes by make me up as an individual person.
